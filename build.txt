1. Run through the "install.txt" instructions to make sure everything is setup.
2. Click on the Terminal in the bottom of the Pycharm IDE to open the versionify virtualenv.
3. From the terminal, run "pyinstaller versionify.py". This will build the versionify.exe in the ~/dist directory.
4. Open the versionify.aip file in "Advanced Installer"
5. From within Advanced Installer, click "Build"
6. From within Advanced Installer, click "Open Output Folder"
7. The "versionify.msi" installer is now in the versionify-SetupFiles directory!
